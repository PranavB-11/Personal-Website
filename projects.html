Pacman AI

Python, PyTorch, Tensorflow

  • Developed a Pacman AI that used an A* algorithm based on the following heuristics: Euclidean Distance, Manhattan Distance, "food", and corners to calculate paths.
  • These distance heuristics were calculated using BFS and an "agent" that would search for an ideal path and send it back to Pacman to follow.
